JavaScript Regular Expressions

.   find a single character, except newline or line terminator \w   find a word character \W   find a non-word character \d   find a digit \D   find a non-digit character \s   find a whitespace character \S   find a non-whitespace character \b   find a match at the beginning/end of a word \B   find a match, but not at the beginning/end of a word \0   find a NULL character
